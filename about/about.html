PROJECT STATEMENT

This radio play was first conceived while the writers were living in Montreal, spending time at Concordia University's 11th floor rooftop greenhouse.

Life conspired to delay the project, but after twists and turns, the writing was completed in 2017. The recording took place at the radio station CJSR in Edmonton in 2018.

The audio design was finally finished in 2019, and released to little fanfare.

PROJECT TEAM

Stefan Opryshko

Director, Writer, Editor
and Audio Engineer

Stefan is an audio engineer and musician living in Toronto. He loves peanut butter, cooking, and snuggling with his cat Bernie.

Larissa Diakiw

Writer

Larissa Diakiw is a writer, living and working in Toronto. She has written for Hazlitt, Brick, the Walrus and Guts. She won a silver 2019 NMA. She publishes Comics and Graphic Essays as Frankie NoOne.

Parker Thiessen

Music

Parker is a filmmaker, sound artist, musician and designer based in Edmonton. His film Midnight Oscillations was inspired by his soundtrack of the same name, which also inspired the music for Caretakers of the Waiting.
